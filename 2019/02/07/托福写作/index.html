Do you agree or disagree with the following statement? People who keep their rooms neat and organized are more successful than those who do not.
neat:整洁

Let us think of a room full of articles for daily use, mop, soap and what else. Even a little mistake would probably make the cleaning a mess innumerable things have to be placed correctly. We will easily make out that ability using in tidying will help to success.

原文：
Rules for flying robots
飞行机器人之规则

Drones need to be encouraged, and people protected
无人机应该受到鼓励，但人们也需要得到保护

Tighter regulations will not hamper troublemakers
再严格的监管也不会阻止麻烦制造者

While testing a drone to detect sharks off a beach in New South Wales last year, Australian lifeguards spotted two young men struggling to swim in the violent surf.
去年，澳大利亚的救生员在新南威尔士州的一个海滩上测试用于探测鲨鱼的无人机时，发现两名年轻男子在波涛汹涌的海浪中奋力地游着。

The drone was dispatched to drop an inflatable pod, which the men used to reach the shore safely.
他们便操纵着无人机将一个充气筏投了下去，这两个人利用这个充气筏安全地抵达了岸边。

Such civilian drones are saviours that have helped rescue mountain-climbers and people trapped by natural disasters.
这种民用无人机是一些人的救星，它可以用来帮助那些等待救援的登山者以及遭受自然灾害的受困者。

They carry emergency medical supplies and organs for transplant.
它们还可以用来运输紧急医疗用品以及用于移植的人体器官。

Apart from saving souls, civilian drones are becoming a good business.
除了救人外，民用无人机正在成为一门好生意。
Goldman Sachs, a bank, reckons that the market will be worth $100bn by 2020 in areas such as surveying, security and delivery.
据高盛银行估计，到2020年，民用无人机在勘测、安全和运输等领域的市场价值将达到1000亿美元。

The trouble is that drones also endanger life and cause disruption, as they did on January 22nd when Newark airport near New York closed briefly after a drone was seen nearby.
但问题是，无人机也会危及生命以及制造混乱，就像1月22日，纽约的纽瓦克机场因为在附近发现了一架无人机而进行了短暂的关闭。

Drone sightings at Gatwick airport near London forced it to shut for 36 hours just before Christmas.
伦敦盖特威克机场的无人机目击事件迫使机场在圣诞节前关闭了36个小时。

Three weeks later a drone closed Heathrow, the world’s third-busiest airport, for an hour. These were hardly the first such incidents.
三周后，一架无人机导致世界上第三繁忙的希思罗机场关闭了一个小时。这种事情不是第一次发生了。

Stockholm’s Arlanda Airport suspended flights in 2017 after spotting a drone. Pilots frequently report near-misses.
2017年，在发现了一架无人机后，斯德哥尔摩的阿兰达机场暂停了航班。飞行员经常报告（与无人机）擦肩而过的事件。

Because they contain metal parts and potentially explosive lithium-ion batteries, drones can badly damage an aircraft in a collision.
由于无人机含有金属部件以及可能会爆炸的锂电池，因此在发生碰撞时会严重地损坏飞机。

They are also used to smuggle contraband across borders and into prisons.
无人机还被用于越境走私违禁品以及劫狱。

In Yemen Houthi rebels recently used a drone to attack the VIP podium of a military parade-ground, reportedly killing six soldiers.
近期，也门的胡赛叛乱分子利用无人机袭击了一个军事阅兵场的高级指挥中心，据报道导致了6名士兵的死亡。
